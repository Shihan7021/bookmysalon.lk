Common Questions

Can I change my plan later?

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time from your salon dashboard settings.

Is there a contract or commitment?

No, our plans are month-to-month. You can cancel your subscription whenever you want with no hidden fees.

How do I get paid for online bookings?

For Online Payments, we integrate with PayHere and KOKO. Credits are settled to your bank account weekly.

Do you offer onboarding support?

Absolutely! Our team will help you set up your services, staff, and initial calendar for free on the Basic and Premium plans.
